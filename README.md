# Sun King Case Study: EB Programs Optimization

## Overview
This repository contains the case study for the 
**Employee Benefits (EB) Programs Optimization**
 at Sun King. The purpose of this case study is to analyze current EB programs, identify opportunities for improvement, and propose data-driven strategies to optimize employee benefits, engagement, and overall organizational impact.

## Objective
The main goals of this case study are:

1. **Analyze Current EB Programs**  
   Evaluate existing employee benefits programs, including healthcare, retirement plans, wellness initiatives, and other perks.

2. **Identify Gaps and Opportunities**  
   Highlight areas of inefficiency, underutilization, or misalignment with employee needs and organizational goals.

3. **Propose Optimization Strategies**  
   Recommend actionable improvements to maximize value for employees while controlling costs for the organization.

4. **Measure Impact**  
   Develop key performance indicators (KPIs) to track the success of proposed optimizations over time.

## Scope
- **Programs Analyzed**: Wellness Programs, Employee Recognition, and Bottleneck challenges.
- **Data Sources**: HRIS data, employee surveys, participation reports, and financial records.
- **Methodologies**: Descriptive analytics, benchmarking, cost-benefit analysis, and predictive modeling.

Methodology

Data Cleaning & Preparation – Ensure consistency and accuracy across all EB data.

Benchmarking – Compare Sun King’s EB programs with industry standards.

Optimization Modeling – Identify the most impactful adjustments to programs.

Recommendations – Actionable steps to optimize EB programs.
